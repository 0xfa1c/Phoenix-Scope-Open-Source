Privacy Policy for Phoenix Scope

Last Updated: 25th January, 2024

Welcome to Phoenix Scope, a dedicated platform designed to streamline the process of finding bug bounty programs from leading platforms such as HackerOne, Intigriti, and Bugcrowd. Our commitment to your privacy and data protection is paramount. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we process, how we handle it, and the measures in place to protect your privacy.

Information We Collect

Phoenix Scope operates on a real-time data fetching model, meaning we do not store personal data of our users. The information processed through our tool includes:

  • Publicly available data from HackerOne, Intigriti, and Bugcrowd regarding bug bounty programs.
  • Non-personal data such as query parameters to customize data fetching, which does not include any user identifiers or sensitive information.

Use of Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of fetching and displaying relevant bug bounty program data from the specified platforms. We do not use this information for any other purposes, such as profiling or marketing.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Phoenix Scope does not share, sell, rent, or trade user information with third parties for their commercial purposes. The data fetched is presented directly to the user without any intermediary storage or processing.

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect the data processed through our tool. However, given the nature of our service (real-time data fetching without storage), the risk associated with data processing is significantly minimized.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our data protection practices.
